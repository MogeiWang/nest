std::vector<double> nest::sinusoidal_gamma_generator::Buffers_::t0_ms_

Beginning of current integration interval in ms.

This is either the most recent spike, or the most recent parameter change, whichever is later. update() must integrate Lambda from t0_ to the current time. The integral from the most recent spike to t0_ is given as Lambda_t0_. Entries are indexed by port, one per target.
